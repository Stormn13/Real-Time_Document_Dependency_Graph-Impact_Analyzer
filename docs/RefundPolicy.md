# Refund Policy – Version 1  
POLICY_ID: REFUND_POLICY  

## Overview  
This is the official refund policy for customer purchases.

## Refund Period  
Customers may request a refund within **14 days** of purchase.  
[REF: REFUND_POLICY]

## Eligible Products  
Only **unopened physical items** are eligible for refunds.

## Exceptions  
Digital goods are **non-refundable** under all circumstances.

## Refund Process  
Customers must submit a refund request **via email** to support@example.com.

## Processing Time  
Refund requests generally take **5–7 business days** to process.
